About Me

I created this website as a hands-on experiment with Claude's new MCP (Model Context Protocol). I'm a Pitzer College Class of '24 graduate, where I majored in Organizational Studies and minored in Data Science. While I have a mildly technical background, my true passions lie in sales and exploring the potential of AI.

I firmly believe that AI integration can transform workflows—not just in sales, but across industries. The possibilities are limitless, and I'm excited to be part of this evolution.

My journey with AI began in the fall of '22 when I started using ChatGPT. Since then, I've been fascinated by its growth and applications. With Claude's MCP, I've unlocked the ability to build a seamless network between the model, my personal device, and the internet. This setup provides end-to-end control over the model's interactions and capabilities.
